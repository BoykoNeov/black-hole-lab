Performance
Scales the geodesic march with the square of resolution; overlays stay sharp. Low also shortens the march — the only setting that trades physical accuracy (a slightly softer photon ring) for speed.
Defaults to 60 — a per-pixel raymarcher gains nothing from a 144 Hz panel's extra frames but pays the full GPU cost of them. Raise it for a smoother pan. Any limit at or above your display's refresh rate does nothing; the browser already caps frames to vsync.
